Chicken Curry

Yummy homemade Chicken curry

Home made chicken curry, goes well with rice, boiled eggs and flavored yoghurt

Ingredients

  • Chciken breast
  • onion
  • red pepper
  • paprika
  • turmeric
  • salt
  • chicken broth

Steps

  1. Cut vegetables
  2. Wash rice
  3. add powder
  4. Cook in electric cooker
  5. Serve hot!
